Welcome to Modest Walrus, a website about the things I cherish: games and music.

## Gaming
I've been playing games for as long as I can remember. From the pixelated chaos of **Doom** and the endlessly satisfying loop of **Tetris**. Furthermore, the grand strategy and multiplayer of **Warcraft II** and the brutal competitiveness of **Counter-Strike**.

Some favorites that have stuck with me over the years:

**Classics & Strategy**
Doom · Tetris · NHL 1995 · Master of Orion · Heroes of Might and Magic · Warcraft II · Age of Empires II · Starcraft

**Shooters & Online**
Counter-Strike · Halo 2 · APB Reloaded · Team Fortress 2

**Open World & Action**
Grand Theft Auto IV · Grand Theft Auto V · Sleeping Dogs · Batman: Arkham City · Red Dead Redemption 2 · Hitman: World of Assassination

**Everything Else**
Portal 2 · Factorio · Hearts of Iron IV

Whether it's building an empire, surviving a zombie apocalypse or just losing hours to a strategy game. I'm here for all of it.
